NoiZe's Arcade Game Solution

How to play

1. Try to reach the water 5 times, lead the character with arrow keys.

2. Try to collect the Heart for extra life.

3. Avoid the enemies they grab your lives!

4. Press E to change enemies.

5. Press P to change player.

6. Press R to reset the game.

7. Press H to remove/display this help.
